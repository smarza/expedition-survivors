# Haldor Stormborn

## Role

The iconic Viking lead and the first character new players meet. Haldor must feel powerful immediately, remain recognizable at small scale, and communicate the game's expedition theme rather than serving as a generic warrior skin.

## Identity

- **Faction:** The Ravenbound.
- **Archetype:** durable close-range leader with directional mobility and automatic mid-range axes.
- **Silhouette:** broad fur mantle, round shield, asymmetrical axe, raven brooch.
- **Palette:** navy wool, iron, copper-blond beard, icy-cyan runes and gold mastery accents.
- **Personality:** fearless and warm; laughs at danger, protects the expedition, remembers every fallen companion.
- **Promise:** easy to learn, satisfying at level one, capable of spectacular axe storms in an advanced build.

## Signature kit

- **Frost Axe:** automatic throwing axe that spins, pierces and can become a critical golden rune axe.
- **Raven Guard:** shield pulse that clears breathing room around Haldor.
- **Ravenstorm:** strategic player-triggered Ultimate that grants brief invulnerability and devastates a large area. Its long cooldown preserves the automatic-combat identity.
- **Oath-Bound:** higher initial health and armor than future characters.

## Future evolution

`Frost Axe + Raven Brooch → Stormfather's Promise`: axes return to Haldor after reaching maximum distance; returning axes carry enemies toward the shield pulse and critical hits call a spectral raven strike.

## Narrative hook

Haldor's homeland was cut from its sea when the Convergence split the coast into floating fragments. He leads expeditions to recover the Echoes needed to rebuild a navigable path between the Ravenbound settlements.
